Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting your next buyer.

I'm stuck in crowded wholesaling markets. Can BuyerBridge help me find new markets? +
Yes. Our permit-backed market search shows you exactly which counties have active builders filing permits. You can identify untapped markets in seconds and start targeting buyers who are actually purchasing land there.
How is this different from a JV partner or Facebook groups? +
Facebook groups are full of unverified buyers and tire-kickers. BuyerBridge uses real permit data to verify that every buyer has actually purchased land and filed building permits. You're matching with proven buyers, not strangers.
How fast can I get matched with buyers? +
Instantly. Submit your deal details and our AI returns ranked buyer matches in under 15 seconds. No waiting, no manual research.
Are the buyers actually verified? +
Every buyer in our database is verified through real building permit filings within the past 12 months. We track 1,500+ data points per buyer including purchase history, permit activity, and buying preferences.
Is this for new or experienced wholesalers? +
Both. New wholesalers use BuyerBridge to find their first market and get matched with buyers instantly. Experienced investors use it to expand into new markets, manage JV deals, and scale their disposition process.
